What is SSA doing right now?


Current Activities


  • Mentorship sessions for FLL teams, including those from Sir Ernest Macmillan SPS
  • Sharing tips, updates, and news on social media
  • Facilitating community discussion (through Discord), on topics such as:
    • Game strategies
    • Design ideas
    • Technical help
    • Non-technical help
    • Outreach ideas
    • Resources
    • Jokes



Upcoming Events and Plans


  • Showcases to the public in malls and other community areas
  • Networking events among Scarborough's tech-related clubs or teams
  • Creating guides for starting new robotics teams



In the Works


  • Network for parts lending and sharing
  • Kickstarting robotics teams
  • Unofficial practise matches
  • Cross-team fundraising events
