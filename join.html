Who can Join?


We target the students, coaches, and mentors of current or potential robotics teams in or around Scarborough. If you match that description, feel free to join us!

Our main form of communication is through Discord. We also have a Google Classroom for students below 13 years old.

If there are issues with joining either platform, or if you have other questions, shoot us an email at scarborough.stem.alliance@gmail.com

You could also simply follow our Instagram and Twitter to get regular tips, updates, and other useful information.
